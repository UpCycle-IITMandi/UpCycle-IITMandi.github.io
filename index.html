About Us

A remote college has its own challenges.

  • Lack of discovery for local vendors
  • No exchange for 2nd hand goods
  • Limited market, hence monopoly
  • No peer service offering
  • Tiresome process of ordering food
  • Disconnect from the big city markets

But the crowd that stays on the campus, the majority of them comes from cities where such services exist. No one is happy to spend more money than required and spend unnecessary time on any activity which they used to do in 3 clicks in their hometown.

Village Square is a one-stop medium to access the entire local market for remote colleges and their campus residents, making the process seamless and hassle-free.

Product

Village Square is a hyperlocal marketplace in the form of an android-based application that is a one-stop medium to access the entire local market, making the process seamless and hassle-free.


We offer several services for organisations that will help the community connect.

Marketplace

Curated local vendors from nearby villages will be listed. Select the shop, pick your food items from the menu, checkout and your food is ordered and ready to deliver.

Classifieds

List goods you don't need anymore and the community has the option to buy it. A second hand marketplace enabling peer-to-peer transactions and reusability of goods.

Self-shops

Set up your own shop anywhere and list them digitally on the application. Take orders from the application grow your business using the applications user base.

Peer-to-peer service offering

Remote institutions have limited service providers for basic tasks. Students can register themselves as freelancers or service providers and start earning money for their skill and service.
